Welcome to Building Interactive Music! We are so excited to have you here.

After pressing the "Continue" button, you will input music to our generator by either choosing a preloaded note sequence, creating your own custom note sequence through our keyboard, or inputting a chord sequence.

Then, our generator will extend your melody and provide two generated music options for you to visualize, listen, and select.
Our generator will then continuously use your selection to generate more music and provide another two options.

Enjoy listening!
